About Me

Photo by Layla Blackshear

I am a copyeditor and technical writer living in Austin, Texas. When I was young, I entertained myself by making up stories in my head, and eventually I started scribbling them down in notebooks. Now I write technical documentation and edit manuscripts for publication. In 2011, I received my masterâ€™s in writing from the New School University, and I was a 2013 Lambda Literary Fellow.

Over the course of my career, I've worked in the newspaper production, book publishing, software, and finance industries as a technical writer, copyeditor, layout designer, and presentation specialist. I am fascinated by the structures in place in the world around my and love figuring out how they work, particularly in the areas of language, psychology, and visual design.

When not working or writing, I can usually be found somersaulting through the air on the flying trapeze or contorting myself into strange shapes on the static trapeze. I like to snuggle on the couch with my spouse, Jill, and our dog, Rizzo, while watching Law and Order: SVU reruns. I also spend a lot of time tweeting and playing with Olive, Margo, and Jace, my three rescue cats.
